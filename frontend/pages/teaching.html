Teaching and editing

Andrea offers critique and feedback on creative writing and essays/reports as well editing for articles and manuscripts. She also provides a tuition service, both one-to-one and in small groups, for either creative writing or English as a second language. Fees are reasonable and if you are interested please contact Andrea.


In addition, Andrea is an occasional tutor for the WEA (Workers Educational Association) in Creative Writing.


Please see contact Andrea for more information on any of the above.
